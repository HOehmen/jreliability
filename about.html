About JReliability

JReliability is a reliability evaluation library developed at Hardware/Software Co-Design, Department of Computer Science at the University of Erlangen-Nuremberg, Germany. The library is tailored for (but not restrictred to) the BDD-based modeling and analysis of complex systems like, e.g., networked embedded systems. Up to now, several publications on international conferences have already been presented that rely on JReliability as the modeling and analysis library. Currently, 3 Ph.D. students as well as some graduate students develop and use this framework.
