About JReliability

JReliability is a reliability evaluation library. The library is tailored for (but not restrictred to) the BDD-based modeling and analysis of complex systems like, e.g., networked embedded systems. JReliability is the base for several publications in journals and at international conferences on design automation for dependable embedded systems.
